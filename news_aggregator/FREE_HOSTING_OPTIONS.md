## 🏆 RECOMMENDED: GitHub Actions

### Why GitHub Actions?

✅ **Completely Free** - No credit card needed
✅ **Easy Setup** - 10 minutes
✅ **Always Runs** - 24/7 even when your PC is off
✅ **Built-in** - No external services to manage
✅ **Logs** - See exactly what's happening
✅ **Security** - Secrets are encrypted
✅ **No Limits** - For personal use

### How Much Does It Cost?

- **Public repository**: 3,000 free action minutes per month (unlimited)
- **Private repository**: 2,000 free action minutes per month
- Your script runs ~30 seconds per day = 15 minutes/month = way under limit
- **Your cost: $0 forever**

### Setup Time: 15 Minutes
